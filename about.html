# bio
    
# ---------------------------------

i grew up in sydney, australia. for my undergrad, i studied civil engineering becoming interested in sustainability and water infrastructure. after graduating, i worked as a dams and hydropower engineer in sydney and london, uk. five years in and just out of a pandemic, i needed a change. i wanted to find more creative and rapid ways of building technology to solve problems for others. that led me to the design engineering program at harvard. ps70 is a chance for me to learn the skills i wish i had been taught in undergrad!

my interests include music, food, wine, basketball, design, architecture, circular economies, industrial ecology, waste, regenerative agriculture, soil health and climate adaptation.
